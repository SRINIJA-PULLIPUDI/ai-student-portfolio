### Research Sources (Perplexity Q&A simulation)

**Q1 — Hiring process at Infosys for B.Tech freshers 2025-26?**  
A: 3-round process — Online Aptitude Test (95 min: Quant + Logical + Verbal), Technical Interview (DSA, OOPs, DBMS, project discussion), HR Interview. Two entry pathways: InfyTQ platform certification or Campus/Off-campus drive.  
*Source: prepinsta.com/infosys/recruitment-process, freshersflow.in/blog/infosys-hiring-process-freshers-2026*

**Q2 — Technical stack Infosys hires for in 2025-26 fresher batch?**  
A: Java (primary language), Python, C++, C#; Spring Boot, React, Node.js; SQL/MySQL/Oracle; Microsoft Azure, AWS basics; AI/ML fundamentals and Infosys Topaz for DSE track; Selenium/JUnit for testing roles.  
*Source: enggwave.com/infosys-hiring-freshers-2026, offcampusjobs4u.com, placementpreparation.io*

**Q3 — Recent news relevant for B.Tech students targeting Infosys 2025-26?**  
A: No layoffs confirmed by CEO Salil Parekh (Apr 2026); 20,000 freshers planned for FY27; Infosys Topaz AI platform scaling; partnerships with OpenAI and Anthropic; 300+ freshers terminated in Feb 2025 for failing Mysuru internal assessment.  
*Source: businesstoday.in, peoplematters.in, outlookbusiness.com (Apr 30 2026), careers360.com (Feb 2025)*

**Q4 — Eligibility criteria for B.Tech 2026 placement at Infosys?**  
A: Minimum 60% / 6.0 CGPA throughout 10th, 12th, and Graduation. Zero active backlogs. Max 2-year education gap. Open to 2024, 2025, 2026 batches. All engineering branches eligible.  
*Source: prepinsta.com/infosys/eligibility-criteria, enggwave.com, offcampusjobs4u.com*

**Q5 — 5 specific preparation tips for a B.Tech 3rd-year targeting Infosys?**  
A: (1) Register on InfyTQ free; (2) Solve 150+ LeetCode problems in Java/Python; (3) Revise OOPs, DBMS, OS, CN; (4) Prepare 3-min project pitch; (5) Register for HackWithInfy coding competition.  
*Source: beincareer.com/infosys-infytq-2026, jobhuntdaily.com/exams/infosys-infytq, placementpreparation.io*
